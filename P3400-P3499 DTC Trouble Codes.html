P3400-P3499 Diagnostic Trouble Codes

This is the listing of P3000-P3499 OBD-II diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs). They almost all start with P34 (e.g. P3413, P342F, etc.), the first three characters P34 designate that they are generic powertrain related codes. The codes below are considered generic because they apply to all makes/models of OBD-II vehicles, although specific diagnosis & repair steps may vary.

We have literally thousands more codes listed on the site, use the links below to go to the other code listings. If you can't find what you need, please use our search or ask a question in the forums.
