P01xx OBD-II Trouble Codes

This is the listing of P01xx OBD-II diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs). They all start with P01 (e.g. P0101, P0140, etc.), the first letter P designates they are powertrain related codes, the next digits 01 indicates that they are fuel & air metering related codes. The codes below are considered generic because they apply to all makes/models of OBD-II vehicles, although specific diagnosis and repair steps may vary.

We have literally thousands more codes listed on the site, use the links below to go to the other code listings. If you can't find what you need, please use our search or ask a question in the forums.
