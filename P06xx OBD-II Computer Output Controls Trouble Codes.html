P06xx OBD-II Trouble Codes (Computer Output)

This is the listing of P06xx OBD-II diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs). They all start with P06 (e.g. P0601, P0670, etc.), the first letter P designates they are powertrain related codes, the next digits 06 indicates they are computer output circuit related codes. The codes below are considered generic because they apply to all makes/models of OBD-II vehicles, although specific diagnosis & repair steps may vary.

We have literally thousands more codes listed on the site, use the links below to go to the other code listings. If you can't find what you need, please use our search or ask a question in the forums.
