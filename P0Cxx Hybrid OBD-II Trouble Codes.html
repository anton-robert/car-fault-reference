P0Cxx Hybrid Trouble Codes

This is the listing of P0C00-P0CFF OBD-II diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs). They all start with P0C, the first letter P designates they are powertrain related codes, the next characters 0C indicates they are generic hybrid related codes. The codes below are considered generic because they apply to all makes/models of OBD-II vehicles, although specific diagnosis & repair steps may vary.

We have literally thousands more codes listed on the site, use the links below to go to the other code listings. If you can't find what you need, please use our search or ask a question in the forums.
