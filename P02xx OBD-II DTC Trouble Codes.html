P02xx Powertrain (Fuel & Air Metering) DTCs

This is the listing of P02xx OBD-II diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs). They all start with P02 (e.g. P0230, P0299, etc.), the first letter P designates they are powertrain related codes, the next digits 02 indicates they are fuel & air metering injector circuit related codes. The codes below are considered generic because they apply to all makes/models of 1996 and newer vehicles, although specific diagnosis & repair steps may vary.

We have literally thousands more codes listed on the site, use the links below to go to the other code listings. If you can't find what you need, please use our search or ask a question in the forums.
